⚠️ Pull Rate Disclaimer
Pull rates used in this calculator are community estimates — Bandai does not publish official pack odds. Rates are sourced from community data and may not perfectly reflect your experience. EV figures are statistical expectations over a large sample; individual boxes will vary significantly.

Card prices are sourced from TCGCSV (a ~24-hour TCGPlayer cache). For manual price verification and additional pull rate research, visit TCGPlayer.

💡 Bulk Pricing Note
Cards priced below $0.50 on TCGPlayer are valued at $0.01 each in this calculator. TCGPlayer's Market price for low-value cards reflects individual single-sale listings that include ~$0.50 of shipping and fees per card — a price that isn't realistically achievable when opening a box full of commons and uncommons. The $0.01 rate approximates the going bulk rate for those cards.
